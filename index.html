Aurelian generates a diagram which maps the flow of your money over the course of a year.

How to Use

  1. Drag input sliders on the left to enter your information.
  2. Drag any node (bar) to compare against another.
  3. Use the diagram to better understand your finances.

Details

  • Aurelian is built with: HTML, CSS, JS, Node, and D3.
  • No backend, no other libraries such as JQuery or React.
  • Fully responsive, works even better on mobile.
